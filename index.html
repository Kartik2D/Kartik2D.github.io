A little bit about me first! I'm Kartik and I'm a generalist. My design philosphy is about using animation principles to help drive the flow of information between a human and a computer. Interaction design and motion design are about bringing principles of real-life charm into our digital world to provide fleeting moments of stimulus. Combine these two fields and you get something with a satisfying and wholesome flavor. I, and many others, know it as juicy design, but I like to think of it as UI/UX with UMAMI. So how exactly do I explore savory flavors in software? Take a look at my exploratory projects below!

Tools I like: After Effects Blender Unity Photoshop Illustrator Affinity Designer Gamemaker Studio Lua Javascript Godot
